Zoom-bot

The Zoom-bot is an interactive navigation and maintence interface that could be implemented on subways and trains all over the world to improve riders experience. It assists various calssifications of users in three parts, maintenance, navigation, and air quality. The Zoom-bot will keep track of train cleanliness and alert maintenance workers of problems. Subway employees will be using the interface to track trains’ cleanliness and location. Riders will find benefits of the system through its air purifier and vaccuming as well as viewing navigation tips. The high fidelity wireframes of the interfaces have been developed. Click here to see the figma project.
