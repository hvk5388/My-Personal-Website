Lowe's Internship

We ran the Item Search and Maintenece tab on the Enterprise Item Portal (vendor website). I designed UI fixes and feature requests under the direction of senior developers. In order to understand the website we ran, I also constructed React.js prototype of the internal data driven website. Through ESLint I gained experience linting our entire code repository by implementing new rules. To improve the onboarding expereince I also created Confluence documentation. At the end of my time at Lowe's, my Hack-a-Thon team was awarded 1st place for designing a Neo4j graph database.
